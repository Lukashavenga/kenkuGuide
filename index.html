Kenku Racial Traits

Mimicry

You can mimic sounds you have heard, including voices. A creature that hears the sounds you make can tell they are imitations with a successful Wisdom (Insight) check opposed by your Charisma (Deception) check. You cannot speak originally, only mimic sounds heard.

Expert Forgery

You can duplicate other creatures' handwriting and craftwork. You have advantage on all checks made to produce forgeries or duplicates of existing objects.

Kenku Training

You are proficient in your choice of two of the following skills: Acrobatics, Deception, Stealth, and Sleight of Hand. Your proficiencies are: Acrobatics and Stealth.

Flight Limitation

Kenku once served a mysterious, powerful entity on another plane of existence. They were stripped of their wings and their original voices as punishment for a crime they committed. You cannot fly.

College of Whispers Features

Psychic Blades (3rd Level)

When you use your Bardic Inspiration to deal damage, you can expend one use of your Bardic Inspiration to deal psychic damage to the target. The damage equals the number you roll on the Bardic Inspiration die.

Current: 2d6 psychic damage using Bardic Inspiration

Words of Terror (3rd Level)

As an action, you can whisper a phrase that only one creature of your choice within 30 feet can hear. The target must make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, it takes 3d6 psychic damage and is frightened until the end of your next turn.

Uses: 1 per short rest

Spellcasting

Spell Attack Bonus: +4
Spell Save DC: 12
Spellcasting Ability: Charisma
Spell Slots: 4 × 1st level, 2 × 2nd level

Cantrips (3 Known)

Vicious Mockery

Casting Time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Components: V Duration: Instantaneous

Effect: You unleash a string of insults laced with subtle enchantments at a creature you can see within range. If the target can hear you (though it need not understand you), it must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or take 1d4 psychic damage and have disadvantage on the next attack roll it makes before the end of its next turn.

At Higher Levels: This spell's damage increases by 1d4 when you reach 5th level (2d4), 11th level (3d4), and 17th level (4d4).

Message

Casting Time: 1 action Range: 120 feet Components: V, S, M (a short piece of copper wire) Duration: 1 round

Effect: You point your finger toward a creature within range and whisper a message. The target (and only the target) hears the message and can reply in a whisper that only you can hear. You can cast this spell through solid objects if you are familiar with the target and know it is beyond the barrier.

Thunderclap

Casting Time: 1 action Range: 5 feet Components: S Duration: Instantaneous

Effect: You create a burst of thunderous sound that can be heard up to 100 feet away. Each creature within 5 feet of you must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take 1d6 thunder damage.

At Higher Levels: This spell's damage increases by 1d6 when you reach 5th level (2d6), 11th level (3d6), and 17th level (4d6).

1st Level Spells (4 Known)

Disguise Self

Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self Components: V, S Duration: 1 hour

Effect: You make yourself—including your clothing, armor, weapons, and other belongings on your person—look different until the spell ends or until you use your action to dismiss it. You can seem 1 foot shorter or taller and can appear thin, fat, or in between. You can't change your body type, so you must adopt a form that has the same basic arrangement of limbs. Otherwise, the extent of the illusion is up to you.

Dissonant Whispers

Casting Time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Components: V Duration: Instantaneous

Effect: You whisper a discordant melody that only one creature of your choice within range can hear, wracking it with terrible pain. The target must make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, it takes 3d6 psychic damage and must immediately use its reaction, if available, to move as far as its speed allows away from you. The creature doesn't move into obviously dangerous ground, such as a fire or a pit. On a successful save, the target takes half as much damage and doesn't have to move.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 1st.

Faerie Fire

Casting Time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Components: V Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

Effect: Each object in a 20-foot cube within range is outlined in blue, green, or violet light (your choice). Any creature in the area when the spell is cast is also outlined in light if it fails a Dexterity saving throw. For the duration, objects and affected creatures shed dim light in a 10-foot radius. Any attack roll against an affected creature or object has advantage if the attacker can see it, and the affected creature or object can't benefit from being invisible.

Healing Word

Casting Time: 1 bonus action Range: 60 feet Components: V Duration: Instantaneous

Effect: A creature of your choice that you can see within range regains hit points equal to 1d4 + your spellcasting ability modifier. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the healing increases by 1d4 for each slot level above 1st.

2nd Level Spells (3 Known)

Crown of Madness

Casting Time: 1 action Range: 120 feet Components: V, S Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

Effect: One humanoid of your choice that you can see within range must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or become charmed by you for the duration. While the target is charmed, a twisted crown of jagged iron appears on its head, and a madness glows in its eyes. The charmed target must use its action before moving on each of its turns to make a melee attack against a creature other than itself that you mentally choose. The target can act normally on its turn if you choose no creature or if none are within its reach.

Knock

Casting Time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Components: V Duration: Instantaneous

Effect: Choose an object that you can see within range. The object can be a door, a box, a chest, a set of manacles, a padlock, or another object that contains a mundane or magical means that prevents access. A target that is held shut by a mundane lock or that is stuck or barred becomes unlocked, unstuck, or unbarred. If the object has multiple locks, only one of them is unlocked. If you choose a target that is held shut with arcane lock, that spell is suppressed for 10 minutes, during which time the target can be opened and shut normally.

Suggestion

Casting Time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: V, M (a snake's tongue and either a bit of honeycomb or a drop of sweet oil) Duration: Concentration, up to 8 hours

Effect: You suggest a course of activity (limited to a sentence or two) and magically influence a humanoid you can see within range that can hear and understand you. Creatures that can't be charmed are immune to this effect. The suggestion must be worded in such a manner as to make the course of action sound reasonable. Asking the creature to stab itself, throw itself onto a spear, immolate itself, or do some other obviously harmful act ends the spell.

Combat Tips (just advice not the rule - you play how ever you like :) )

Positioning

Stay 30-60 feet from enemies to maximise spell effectiveness while avoiding melee combat. Use your movement to maintain optimal distance and line of sight.

Primary Actions

  • Vicious Mockery: Primary cantrip for damage + debuff
  • Dissonant Whispers: High damage + forced movement
  • Bardic Inspiration: Support allies with d6 bonus
  • Words of Terror: Frighten key enemies

Weapons

  • Light Crossbow: +5 to hit, 1d8+3 piercing (range 80/320)
  • Rapier: +5 to hit, 1d8+3 piercing (finesse)
  • Dagger: +5 to hit, 1d4+3 piercing (thrown 20/60)

Emergency Tactics

  • Healing Word: Bonus action to revive unconscious allies
  • Suggestion: Remove enemies from combat temporarily
  • Faerie Fire: Group support for advantage on attacks

Roleplay Tips (just advice not the rule - you play how ever you like :) )

Communication Through Mimicry

You can only communicate by mimicking sounds, voices, and phrases you've heard before. Build a "voice bank" of useful phrases, sounds, and voices from NPCs and party members.

Building Your Voice Bank

  • Repeat important phrases from party members
  • Mimic NPC voices for deception
  • Collect environmental sounds (door creaks, animal calls)
  • Layer different voices to express complex ideas

Personality Traits

  • Shy but Intelligent: Observant and cautious
  • Support-Focused: Prefers helping others succeed
  • Curious: Fascinated by new sounds and voices
  • Adaptable: Good at blending into different social situations

Expressing Complex Ideas

Use layered mimicry to communicate complex thoughts:

  • Combine multiple voices for conversations
  • Use environmental sounds for emphasis
  • Mimic emotional tones from different speakers
  • Create "composite" phrases from multiple sources

Equipment & Gear

Weapons

Rapier

Damage: 1d8 piercing

Weight: 2 lb.

Properties: Finesse

Description: A sword with a slender, sharply pointed blade, designed for thrusting attacks. The finesse property allows you to use your Dexterity modifier instead of Strength for attack and damage rolls.

Light Crossbow

Damage: 1d8 piercing

Weight: 5 lb.

Properties: Ammunition (range 80/320), loading, two-handed

Description: A small, one-handed crossbow. Loading property means you can fire only one piece of ammunition when you use an action, bonus action, or reaction to fire it, regardless of the number of attacks you can normally make.

Dagger

Damage: 1d4 piercing

Weight: 1 lb.

Properties: Finesse, light, thrown (range 20/60)

Description: A short blade that can be wielded in one hand. Its light property allows for two-weapon fighting, and it can be thrown as a ranged weapon.

Armor

Leather Armor

AC: 11 + Dex modifier

Weight: 10 lb.

Stealth: No disadvantage

Description: The breastplate and shoulder protectors of this armor are made of leather that has been stiffened by being boiled in oil. The rest of the armor is made of softer and more flexible materials.

Musical Instruments

Lyre

Weight: 2 lb.

Description: A small, harp-like instrument with strings stretched across a U-shaped frame. Perfect for a bard's spellcasting focus and intimate performances.

Drum

Weight: 3 lb.

Description: A percussion instrument consisting of a skin stretched over a circular frame. Ideal for rhythm work and can be played with hands or sticks.

Viol

Weight: 1 lb.

Description: A bowed string instrument, smaller than a violin. Produces haunting, ethereal tones perfect for a College of Whispers bard.

Adventuring Gear

Backpack

Weight: 5 lb.

Description: A leather pack with straps for securing equipment during travel.

Bedroll

Weight: 7 lb.

Description: A thick blanket and sleeping fur for resting during travel.

Disguise Kit

Description: This pouch of cosmetics, hair dye, and small props lets you create disguises that change your physical appearance. Works well with your Disguise Self spell.

Crossbow Bolts (20)

Weight: 1.5 lb.

Thieves' Tools

Leveling Priorities

Level 5 Priorities

  • Font of Inspiration: Bardic Inspiration recharges on short rest
  • 3rd Level Spells: Access to Counterspell, Hypnotic Pattern
  • Improved Cantrips: Vicious Mockery becomes 2d4 damage

Level 6 Priorities

  • Mantle of Whispers: Capture the shadow of a humanoid that dies within 30 feet
  • Additional Magical Secrets: Learn 2 spells from any class
  • Recommended Secrets: Counterspell, Fireball, or Healing spells
